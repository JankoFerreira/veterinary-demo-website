Who We Are

Personalised Service, Made to Measure

Tailor Made Window Decor was built on a simple idea: window decor should be fitted to the home, not the other way around. We measure on site, talk through fabrics and finishes in person, and make every order to your exact dimensions.

The result is a stylish, lasting finish — blinds that sit flush, curtains that fall the way they should, and shutters built to last. No off-the-shelf compromises, just a service shaped entirely around your space.

  • One-on-one consultations at your home
  • Made-to-measure for every window, every time
  • Premium fabrics and stylish, lasting finishes
