Who We Are

Kind Care From Nose To Tail

Harbor Paws Veterinary Clinic is a fictional, locally owned animal clinic built around calm appointments, clear communication and practical care for dogs and cats. We take time to explain what we see, what it means and what your options are.

From puppy and kitten visits to senior pet support, our team focuses on prevention, comfort and follow-through. No rushed handoffs, just thoughtful care shaped around your pet's needs.

  • Low-stress appointments for dogs and cats
  • Wellness, dental, diagnostic and routine surgical care
  • Friendly follow-up from a team that knows your pet
